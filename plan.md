## 6. OB/GYN Reasoning Framework

Teach universal logic:

1. Determine maternal stability  
2. Determine fetal well-being  
3. Identify pregnancy trimester and gestational age  
4. Apply guideline-based diagnostic criteria  
5. Know “when to deliver”—the most important OB decision  
6. For GYN emergencies: rule out ectopic first  
7. For fetal monitoring: resuscitate uterus → reassess → escalate  
8. For postpartum: check hemorrhage causes in correct sequence (tone, trauma, tissue, thrombin)

Pitfalls:
- Misclassifying Category II vs III tracings  
- Incorrect timing for antenatal steroids  
- Missing magnesium sulfate indications  
- Wrong interpretation of GDM thresholds  
- Failure to rule out ectopic pregnancy early
